Add an external source

How to add external sources

In order to add your professional camera, a drone, or even to ingest an OBS stream into the Dazzl platform, you can follow these steps:

  1. Login into the Dazzl platform. You will then be able to create a new event, or select the event on which you want to add an external source.
  2. Select the tab "sources", and choose "external sources".
  3. Click on the red button "Add a new source".
  4. Give a name to the source which you are adding. You can also add a description, to better identify the device or the person who will use it. Then, confirm your creation.
  5. By clicking on the "copy-past" button, you can then retrieve your RTMP address and key. Use it into you camera, drone or OBS platform. 

 

 

When the new source will be well ingested and well connected to the platform, the light next to the source name will turn green, and a window will appear in the "Production" tab, with the name of your source and the video live captured.

 

The recommended encoding parameters are :

  • 720p 30fps @ 2.5Mbs
  • 1080p 30fps @ 6Mbs
