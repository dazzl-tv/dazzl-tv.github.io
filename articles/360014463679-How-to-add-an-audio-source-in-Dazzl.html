How to add an audio source in Dazzl

Whether you want to use music from your computer in Dazzl, or integrate sound from your external sound card, you'll find the answers in this article.

Embedding sound from an external sound card

To do this, make sure your sound card is connected and recognized by your computer, then follow these steps:

  • Start a videocast, in the "Sources"/"Videocast" tab.
  • Click on the camera that appears to the right of your search bar.
  • Go to the audio settings, and select your sound card.
  • Go back to the Dazzl control room, dissociate the audio from the video, and select your videocast as the audio source.

Your event now uses the sound of your sound card.

Integrate music from your computer

To integrate a music, you must first create a virtual sound card using one of the following softwares (free) :

Mac: https://github.com/mattingalls/Soundflower/releases

Windows: https://www.vb-audio.com/Cable/index.htm

Once set up :

  • Launch a videocast, in the "Sources"/"Videocast" tab.
  • Click on the camera that appears to the right of your search bar.
  • Go to the audio settings, and select your virtual sound card.
  • Go back to the Dazzl control room, dissociate the audio from the video, and select your videocast as the audio source.

Your event now uses the sound of your sound card.
