Videocast to your own event

How to ingest your own contribution

As the producer, you can contribute to your own event using your webcam, or any other device connected to you computer.

First, make sure that your audio and video inputs are properly configured. You can connect a microphone and a camera/webcam. Set up these devices in your browser settings.

 

 

Once this is done, select the "source" tab, then "Videocast", and click on the camera logo.

Wait for the image to appear and click on "start videocast".

A wheel will start turning, wait until it has disappeared.

You can then go back to your control room, your new source has appeared there.
