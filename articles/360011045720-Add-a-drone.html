Add a drone

Since there are multiple brands and models of drones, there are different ways to connect them to the Dazzl platform.

But overall, the following steps should always be followed:

  • Connect the drone to its support/appliance.
  • Retrieve the RTMP address, as shown in this article.
  • Use this RTMP address to connect the drone to the dazzl platform.

Here's an example for connecting a DJI drone:

Go to the settings "...", then "Select Live Broadcast Platform".

Click on "Custom", enter the Dazzl RTMP address and start your Live.

 

If you have any problem to connect your drone, don't hesitate to contact us
