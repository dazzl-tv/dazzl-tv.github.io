Add your own testcard

How to add your personalized Testcard?

Whether it is to perform your tests, for waiting times in the live or to switch quickly in case of source problems, we advise you to add one or more testcard which corresponds to your brand or your event.
This will allow you to realize transitions between two periods or your fans to wait while a technical problem is solved.

To add your own testcard, click on "Media", "Waiting screen" and then "Upload an image".

You will then be invited to add your own picture. Be sure to respect the following screen resolution: 1280x720 pixels.

You may need to refresh the page to see your new testcard.
