Use the Dazzl Intercom

Dazzl Platform and app offer you the possibility to communicate directly with your iOS and Android contributors using the intercom feature.

 

 

First of all, make sure to well add your contributors, using the corresponding tab.

When they appear on your video sources, you can then automatically communicate with them : simply press the space bar, this logo become green :

 

 

Please note that it is a one-way communication : Their microphone will be use as an audio input for the live and not to communicate with you.

To receive the communication, they have to allow it using the cross on their screen and clicking on the headphones logo.
