Is it possible to publish on Instagram using Dazzl?

Instagram does not officially offer publishing a stream (RTMP) on its platform other than using its mobile app.

Even if it is possible to bypass this limitation, we are committed to Instagram's policies and therefore do not propose the possibility to share a live stream on this network.
