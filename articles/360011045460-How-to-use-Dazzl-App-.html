How to use Dazzl App ?

Dazzl App

It is very easy to use the Dazzl application.

First you have to :

  1. Download the Dazzl app on your smartphone. Click here for iOS and here for Android.
  2. Create your account and an event if it is your live, or have been added as a contributor if it is someone else's live (see here).

You will then be able to join the event by logging in and clicking on the camera at the bottom of your screen.

Select the event you want to join, then turn your smartphone into landscape mode to start sharing your video.
When the borders of the screen appear red, your video is currently being streamed live.
